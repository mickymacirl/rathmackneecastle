Sir Thomas  de Rositer

Rathmacknee Castle, the ruins of which still remain, was built by Sir Thomas de Roster in 1170, when the family settled there under Strongbow. They came to England with William the Conqueror, and located near Wellington, in Somerset, where the name is frequent to this day.
